Project 1

Innovation Social Entrepreneurship(ISE)/
Values In Action(VIA) Project

This is an Annual programme planned by our school where groups from cohort would need think of a solution with a given topic. And our topic was "Improving a life of an elderly".
So our group came up with a solution of advanced 4 legged crutches where it have many technology functions.
This project gave me an opportunity to visit Taiwan and attend the "Design For Change Taiwan Be The Change Global Conference" with another 9 schoolmates.
This During the conference students from different countries presented how they helped the people from their country. From there I learnt that there is no age restriction to who can help the others' make their lives better and motivate the older ones to also want to be like them and help the others.
